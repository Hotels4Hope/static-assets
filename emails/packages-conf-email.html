Hotel Age Policy
Please note that you must be 21 years old or older to check-in with the Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach. If the guest name on the reservation is for a person that is not 21 or older please contact us immediately. If someone in your party is over 21 we can add their name to your reservation so they can check in for your party. Note that their name must be added to your reservation prior to arrival. To update this information please contact Hotels for Hope at 512-691-9555 or reservations@hotelsforhope.com.
Where Are My Festival Passes?
You will collect your festival passes at our Hotel Package Check In table after you check in. The table will be located in the hotel lobby next to the concierge desk Friday, April 12th between 9am and 5pm. After 5pm please ask the front desk for your packet.
Festival Information & Schedule
  • Festival Dates: Friday, April 12th - Sunday, April 14th
  • Festival Address: 1100 Seabreeze Blvd., Ft. Lauderdale, FL 33316
  • Festival Gates Open (music begins shortly after): 12:00 PM on Friday - Sunday
GA Entrance & Arrival Information
The GA entrance is near the north end of the festival, just south of the DC Alexander Lot and across the street from the Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale.
VIP Entrance & Arrival Information
The VIP entrance is near the southwestern end of the festival, at Seabreeze Blvd and the northern side of the B Ocean Resort, 1140 Seabreeze Blvd, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33316. There will also be a dedicated VIP lane at the main entrance located at the north end of the festival.

Important Firefly Package Information
FireFly wristbands will be available for pick up at the VIP CREDENTIALS DESK located in the lobby of Dover Downs Friday - Sun 11am - 8pm. Additional information regarding your wristband will be provided upon pick up. For additional wristband or ticketing assistance contact support@festivalticketing.com
Important Hotel Information
Dover Downs will be providing a separate line at the Front Desk for all Packages. On arrival please follow the signs as you enter the hotel. Again, rooms are guaranteed for check-in starting at 3:00PM, if you arrive early they will do their best to get you in earlier as needed.
In case you would like to reach out to the hotel directly at any time or for an additional day of arrival assistance, you can also contact the Hotel Director, Gordon Basht, who is overseeing all VIP and Package Stays for Firefly at 302-857-2104 or gbasht@doverdowns.com
