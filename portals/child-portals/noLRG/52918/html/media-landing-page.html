Book Your Accommodations

As in other world championship GP events, accommodation for the Media will be arranged and guarantee your rooms upon your attending the Dutch GP in Zandvoort.

*We will accept the requests on a first-come-first-served basis as the number of rooms is limited.

*Please note that this assistance is limited to 'Individual media representatives only. We are not able to assist large groups, travel agencies and TV crews.
