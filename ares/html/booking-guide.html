Booking Guide

Simple steps to help you make the most of your membership!

Access

If you are a new user, to activate your membership click REGISTER, enter your personal details, confirm your password and you’ll receive immediate access.

If you are an existing member, just click LOGIN and enter your email address and password to access your exclusive rates.

Start Your Search

You’re now ready to start searching over 600,000 hotels with unbeatable rates.

Simply enter your destination, travel dates, number of rooms required and people staying, and hit the search button.

You can also filter by the property type, amenities, rating and preferred currency.

Select Your Hotel

You’ll find additional filters to the left of the hotel listings, where you can sort by savings and price. You can also update your search terms here.

In the top header you’ll find links that navigate to other useful pages, such as your profile, our Customer Service contact info and your RESBEAT Rewards program.

And don’t forget to take note of the significant savings you’ll be making on other travel sites!

Hotel Details

By clicking Book Rooms, you’ll be able to see more specific information on each hotel, such as its location, amenities, reviews and room options.

Also, look out for how many RESBEAT Rewards you can earn from earn booking!

Secure Your Room

Once you’ve chosen your preferred room, you’ll be asked to enter personal and payment details. You can make a reservation for someone else – just make sure you include that person's name and information as the guest. You can also have a different name and address for the guest and for the credit card details.

You also have the option to add an additional email to be notified of the reservation, if required.

This page also contains further details on the hotel’s policies and payment terms. Please review before making your secure purchase.

Confirmation

You’re all set!

Once your payment has been approved, your reservation will be confirmed immediately. You’ll also receive a booking number. Please reference this number if you need to speak to a member of our team.

A confirmation email containing your booking details will be sent to the primary email address you supplied when making your reservation. You’ll also be able to download a receipt.

Your total RESBEAT Rewards earned will also be confirmed. These will be redeemable 48 hours after checkout. Keep a lookout for an email notifying you when these have been added to your Rewards account.

If you have any queries about your reservation, you can check our Frequently Asked Questions. Our Customer Service team are also on hand to help.
